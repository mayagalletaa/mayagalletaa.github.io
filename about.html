A B O U T

Lab25 is an innovative and highly motivated Center of Cognitive and Behavioral Science located
in the Faculty of Psychology from the National Autonomous University of Mexico in Mexico City.

Our main goal is to contribute to a understanding of humans and non-human organisms
behavior as an adaptative response to the structure of the enviroment they live in.

More specifically, we focus on conducting research in terms of:
"Reinforcement Learning, Intertemporal Choice, Risky Choice,
Perceptual Decission Making, Timing, Game Theory, Categorization and Bayesian Modeling"
